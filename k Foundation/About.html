About Us

Kuwane Foundation (KF) is a Not for Profit Organisation (NPC/NPO) registered in 2021 to address the developmental needs of rural communities. However, some of the projects were started in 2020 as efforts by individuals to contribute to addressing poverty. The socio-economic status of ordinary villagers has not changed as people continue to struggle. The South Africa economy has been struggling and as such unemployment, poverty and inequality has not improved. The COVID-19 pandemic has made things worse. Eastern Cape is the poorest province in South Africa with high levels of unemployment and poverty. This means people continue to struggle both socially and economically.

The foundation seeks to mobilise resources and partner with other organisation to bring about change, development and empowerment in the rural areas. This will be done by identifying key projects to implement in areas of employment, food security, health, education, sports and recreation and environment. The foundation partners with other organisations, traditional leaders and communities to implement its projects. Although the initiatives have been started in one village, the aim is to expand to more villages in Joe Gqabi District Municipality with long term goal to expand to more villages in the Eastern Cape Province.
