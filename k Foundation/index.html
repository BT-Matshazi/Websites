our story

Kuwane Foundation

Kuwane Foundation seeks to address poverty and underdevelopment in the rural Eastern Cape Province in South Africa through combination of community based initiatives. The foundation works with other stakeholders and individuals to make a difference and bring about sustainable change.

Our Hero

Luvuyo Mamela

My name is Luvuyo Mamela, I am an Amazulu Football Club Player. I support the work of the Kuwane Foundation. I encourage you to all make donations and support in any other way possible so that the foundation can make a greater impact in addressing poverty and underdevelopment in Eastern Cape. My Foundation, LAM is partnering with Kuwane Foundation.
