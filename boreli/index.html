About

Boreli General Trading

Boreli General Trading (“BGT”) was established in 2018 when it began furniture retail from its mega-store in germiston, Johannesburg, South Africa.

BGT is one of the most innovative, fastest growing luxury brands in the home furnishings marketplace. We manufacture brand new stylist and quality home furnitures from the start to finish. Over the past few years, we have successfully grown our retail footprint in South Africa from 1 to 2 stores. Our flagship store – germiston – pretoria west– offers customers in excess of 5 000sqm of fabulous household furniture, satisfying our customers needs for all rooms in the home. This flagship store has recently been refurbished and offers the groups most extensive range of furniture.

Our ability to innovate and integrate products and categories with a completely authentic and distinctive point of view, is the platform for our continued long-term growth. We continue positioning our Company to create and deliver a lifestyle beyond the four walls of the home. Our unique product development, go-to-market and supply chain capabilities, together with our significant scale, enable us to offer a compelling combination of design, quality and value that we believe is unparalleled in the marketplace. We pride ourselves on the largest range of local and imported household furniture, most of which is readily available. We partner only with leading manufacturers and suppliers, with proven track records to ensure only the best quality is delivered to your home.

Our product range and exclusive lines offer consumers product differentiation at affordable prices and real value for money. We understand the value of a customer and we pride ourselves on providing customers with service excellence, giving customers the most unforgettable and rewarding retail shopping experience.
